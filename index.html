Sweet Potato Fries

  • Time: 33 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
sweet potatoes 2
soybean oil 3 tablespoons
sea salt 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1: Position rack in upper third of oven and preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Spray baking sheet with non-stick spray.
Step 2: Place sweet potatoes and soybean oil in large bowl, toss lightly. Sprinkle with salt.
Step 3:Arrange potatoes in a single layer on prepared baking sheet, being sure not to overcrowd.
Step 4: Bake until tender and golden brown, turning occasionally. Cooking time is 18 to 24 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before serving.

BBQ Pork

  • Time: 4hrs 45 min
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
can beef broth 14 ounce
boneless pork rib 3 pounds
BBQ sauce 18 ounce

Step 1: Pour can of beef broth into slow cooker, and add boneless pork ribs. Cook on High heat for 4 hours, or until meat shreds easily. Remove meat, and shred with two forks. It will seem that it's not working right away, but it will.
Step 2: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Transfer the shredded pork to a Dutch oven or iron skillet, and stir in barbeque sauce.
Step 3:Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through.

Banana Split

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
bananas 4
ice cream 12 scoops
cherries 4
chocolate flavored syrup
whipped cream

Step 1: Arrange two banana halves in each of 4 ice cream dishes. Top each with three scoops ice cream.
Step 2: Drizzle with Nesquik.
Step 3:Top with whipped cream and a cherry. Serve immediately.
